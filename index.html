      FAQ

      Clear answers before you begin.

      What is SkillGapAI?

      SkillGapAI is an AI-powered career readiness platform that helps users identify missing skills and follow a role-based roadmap.

      How does the skill assessment work?

      Users choose a target role, level, region, and goal. The platform compares their path against industry expectations and creates a roadmap preview.

      Is it for students or professionals?

      Both. It is useful for students, fresh graduates, working professionals, freelancers, and career switchers.

      Can colleges use it?

      Yes. Colleges can use SkillGapAI to identify employability gaps and guide students before placements.

      Does it guarantee a job?

      No. It provides guidance, skill direction, and readiness insights. It does not guarantee employment or salary.

      How are learning paths created?

      Learning paths are based on target roles, missing skills, practical projects, and readiness milestones.
