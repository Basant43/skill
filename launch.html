Scanner Reports Roadmap

Zero-budget launch plan

Launch as a service first. Turn it into AI later.

Use the website to attract users, manually create 10 reports, learn what they value, then automate the repeated parts.

Week 1

Validate

Share the website with students and job seekers. Ask what career they want and what confuses them most.

Week 2

Manual reports

Create simple PDF reports for early users using your research and design skill. Charge a small amount when possible.

Week 3

Package the offer

Turn repeated answers into templates: skill map, roadmap, project ideas, resume tips, and portfolio proof.

Week 4

Automate

Add forms, payment, email delivery, and later AI-generated monthly updates when users prove demand.

Founder action

Your first goal is not technology. It is proof that people want this.

Collect 20 interested users, make 5 free reports, try selling 3 paid reports, then decide what to automate.
